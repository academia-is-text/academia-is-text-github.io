What is the AIT Keyword Context Report

Our Keyword Context Report deconstructs your document into a list of non-trivial key terms, ranks them by number of occurences, and performs a "fuzzy" search for each word stems in your text. The resulting report is a comprehensive catalog of the context of each recurring term in your document.
